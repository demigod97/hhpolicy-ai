Policy Title: Remote Work Guidelines for Executives
Effective Date: September 19, 2025
Department: Executive Leadership

1. PURPOSE
This policy establishes guidelines for executive remote work arrangements and ensures business continuity at the leadership level.

2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all executive-level employees including C-suite members, VPs, and senior directors.

3. GUIDELINES
- Executives must maintain regular communication with their teams
- All executive meetings should have hybrid options available
- Critical decisions require in-person presence when possible

4. APPROVAL PROCESS
All executive remote work arrangements must be approved by the CEO or Board of Directors.
